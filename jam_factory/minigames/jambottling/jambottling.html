Instructions:

Click and hold the lever next to the funnel to fill the bottle with jam. Let go when it's full, but don't let it overflow!

Controls:

FILL - press and hold left mouse button with your mouse on the lever.

STOP - let go of the left mouse button or move the mouse away from the lever.
